## Core Modules

### 🎓 Registry — Student & Staff

Admission day shouldn't be the start of a paper trail that gets harder to follow every year after. In Deskwise, a student's record — photo, guardian contacts, emergency details, documents — stays with them from the day they join to the day they graduate, and it's searchable in seconds instead of buried in a filing cabinet. Staff records live right alongside, so HR isn't a separate system either.

**Students**
- Full admission profiles — photos, guardian contacts, emergency info, document storage
- Digital admission forms and enrollment tracking
- Transfer requests and processing
- Live enrollment analytics by grade, gender, and section

**Staff**
- Qualification records, contracts, attendance history, leave tracking
- Role-based access tied to staff profiles

---

### 💰 Finance

Fee collection is usually where the most time gets lost — chasing dues by phone, reconciling cash by hand, waiting until month-end to know where things stand. Set your fee structure into Deskwise once, and it takes over the rest: invoices raise themselves, receipts print automatically, and outstanding dues get flagged without anyone having to go looking for them.

- **Fee structure** — class-wise fee heads, custom configurations
- **Invoicing** — auto-generated bills and receipts on every payment
- **Scholarships** — automatic discounts and waiver management
- **Due alerts** — outstanding dues flagged automatically, no manual chasing
- **Expenses** — expenditure tracking, category-wise reports
- **Wallet system** — staff advances and salary deductions
- **Reports** — income/expenditure summaries, collection analytics

---

### 📚 Academics — Exams & Marks

A teacher's evening shouldn't disappear into a calculator and a stack of blank report cards. Marks get entered once, in Deskwise — grades and GPA are worked out automatically from there, and a polished report card, transfer certificate, or character certificate is a click away, in whichever design fits your school.

- **Subjects** — per grade/section management, teacher assignments
- **Grading scales** — customizable GPA ranges, symbols, credit hours
- **Marks entry** — bulk CSV import, per-student or class-wise entry
- **Report cards** — auto-generated marksheets with GPA calculation
- **Exam schedules** — timetables, seating plans, admit cards
- **Certificates** — 9 professionally designed templates (transfer, character, bonafide, and more), PDF export in one click

---

### 👨‍💼 Staff Management & Payroll

Payroll shouldn't run on memory, or on a manual count of who showed up each day. Because attendance and payroll live in the same system, salary calculations trust the actual clock-in record — so leave deductions and payouts match reality, and nobody has to reconstruct a month from memory at pay time.

- **Attendance** — daily mark-in/mark-out, class-wise trend charts, GPS-verified clock-in
- **Leave management** — request workflow, approval tracking
- **Payroll** — automatic paid-leave / loss-of-pay calculation from attendance data
- **Performance** — staff ranking and evaluation tracking
- **Web access** — invite staff to the web portal with role-based access
- **Class teacher management** — per-teacher grade/section assignment
- Every calculation logged in the 90-day audit trail

### 🚌 Transport & GPS

"Where's the bus?" is a question every school office answers a dozen times a day. With routes and stops mapped ahead of time and the fleet visible on a live map, that question mostly answers itself — and because the map works offline too, a patchy signal on the route doesn't break it.

- **Routes & stops** — define routes, assign pickup points
- **Fleet management** — bus assignment, capacity tracking
- **Student assignment** — per-student bus and stop records
- **Offline GPS map** — live fleet visualization using cached map tiles, no connectivity required

---

### ✅ Multi-Step Approval Workflow

Some decisions are too significant for one person to make alone — waiving a fee, letting a staff member go, changing a salary. Deskwise routes these through an approval chain you set up in advance, so the right people sign off before anything takes effect, and there's always a clear record of exactly what changed and who approved it.

- **Multi-step chains** — define approver sequences with step order
- **Threshold-based routing** — auto-route requests based on amount, action type, or role
- **Before/after preview** — reviewers see exactly what changed before deciding
- **Covered actions:** student/staff deletion, salary changes, transport edits, discount thresholds, scholarship discounts, grading scale updates, promotion settings, calendar events
- **Escalation & forwarding** — requests progress through the chain automatically
- **Audit trail** — every decision logged with timestamp, actor, and remarks

### 🌐 Web Portal

Not everything happens on the office computer. The web portal brings the same system to teachers, principals and parents, wherever they are — on a phone, a home laptop, or a shared family device.

**Staff** — mark attendance and enter marks without waiting for the school computer to be free.
- **My Class** — view and edit student details (roll no, DOB, address, blood group, etc.)
- **Student Attendance** — mark Present/Absent/Late/Holiday, bulk actions, save
- **Marks Entry** — enter and manage exam marks
- **Own Attendance** — view and mark daily attendance
- **Leave** — apply for leave
- **Approvals** — approve or reject requests assigned to you
- **Notices & Notifications** — stay informed
- **Dashboard** — personal tasks, class overview

**Principal** — the same oversight as the desktop dashboard, from anywhere.
- **Dashboard** — KPIs, finance trends, attendance analytics, grade/subject performance
- **Students** — manage admissions and records
- **Attendance** — student and staff attendance overview
- **Academic** — subjects by grade, staff list, grade scores
- **Exams & Finance** — exam setup and fee management
- **Approvals** — full approval center
- **Notices** — create, edit, pin, delete announcements
- **Staff Access** — assign web roles, invite, revoke
- **Leave Requests** — manage staff leave
- **Activities** — admin activity log
- **Settings** — profile, security, academic, ID card config

**Parent** — no app to install, just a login.
- **Overview** — student info, guardian details, quick stats
- **Fees** — invoices, payment history, outstanding amounts
- **Attendance** — summary cards + calendar grid
- **Progress** — exam results with percentages, grades, GPA, and rank

### 🛡️ Role-Based Permissions

A teacher and an accountant shouldn't be looking at the same screen. Every role in Deskwise sees exactly what's relevant to their job and nothing more, and that rule is enforced the same way everywhere — whether they're on the school computer or logging in from home.

- **Clear role hierarchy** — from Owner and Principal down to Staff, with Admin, Vice Principal, Accountant, and Coordinator roles unique to each school
- **50+ fine-grained permissions**, so access can be tailored precisely, not just role-by-role
- **Enforced everywhere** — every page and action, on desktop and web, checked against the same permission map
- Class-teacher status and grade/section assignments are picked up automatically when staff log in

### 📷 CCTV & Dashboard

Security cameras and school management software usually live on two separate screens in two separate rooms. Deskwise brings your existing cameras into the same dashboard your front-desk staff already have open, alongside the day's attendance and collection numbers.

- **Live camera feeds** — DVR and IP camera integration
- **Secondary dashboard** — multi-monitor support for reception/admin desks
- **Real-time stats** — live student count, today's attendance, collections at a glance

> [!NOTE]
> Deskwise provides the viewer interface only. Cameras and recording hardware remain the school's property and responsibility. No CCTV data is stored on Deskwise servers.

---

### ☁️ Cloud Sync

The internet going down shouldn't mean the school day stops. Deskwise runs offline by default and quietly catches up the moment a connection returns — only what's changed gets sent, so even a slow connection is enough.

- **Offline-first** — full operations without internet, always
- **Smart sync** — only changed data is transmitted, not full snapshots
- **Conflict resolution** — last-write-wins with timestamp logic preserving data integrity
- **Multi-device** — sync across licensed devices on the same plan
- **Isolated by school** — cloud-side security ensures one school's data is never visible to another
